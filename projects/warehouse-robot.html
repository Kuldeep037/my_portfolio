Project Overview

The Autonomous Mobile Robot (AMR) for warehouse operations is designed to revolutionize inventory management by automating the movement of goods within large warehouses. This robot navigates independently through complex warehouse environments, scans barcodes, and efficiently transports items between storage locations.

The system uses state-of-the-art LiDAR sensors to create detailed maps of the facility and precise localization. Computer vision algorithms allow the robot to identify items and read barcodes, while advanced path planning ensures collision-free movement around shelving units, workers, and other obstacles.
